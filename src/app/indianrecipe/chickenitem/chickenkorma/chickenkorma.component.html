Chicken Korma (Dhabha Style Chicken Korma Recipe)


Prep time: 25-30 minutes
Cooking time: 30-40 minutes
Serves: 4-5 people
  

Chicken Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 kg boneless, skinless chicken thighs, cut into 1-inch pieces
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon ginger, minced
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon ground coriander
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper (optional)
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 1/2 cup cashew nuts, soaked in water for at least 30 minutes, then drained
  • 1/4 cup heavy cream
  • 1/4 cup chopped cilantro
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

Preparing the Chicken:

  1. Heat the olive oil in a large skillet or pot over medium heat. Add the chicken and cook until browned on all sides. Add the onion, garlic, and ginger to the skillet and cook until softened, about 5 minutes. Add the cumin, coriander, turmeric, garam masala, and cayenne pepper (if using) to the skillet and cook for 1 minute more. Add the yogurt to the skillet and stir to combine. Cover the skillet and reduce the heat to low. Simmer for 20 minutes, or until the chicken is cooked through.
    While the chicken is simmering, puree the cashew nuts in a blender or food processor until smooth. Add the cashew nut puree and heavy cream to the skillet and stir to combine. Bring the sauce to a simmer and cook for 5 minutes, or until thickened slightly. Stir in the cilantro and season with salt and pepper to taste. Serve with rice or naan bread.

Tips:

  • For a richer flavor, you can marinate the chicken in yogurt and spices for a few hours or overnight before cooking.
  • If you don't have cashew nuts, you can use another type of nut, such as almonds or walnuts. Just be sure to soak them in water for at least 30 minutes before blending.
  • To make the sauce ahead of time, simply simmer the chicken in the yogurt and spices until cooked through. Then, let the sauce cool completely and store it in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 2 days. When you're ready to serve, simply reheat the sauce and add the cashew nut puree and heavy cream.
  • You can also add other vegetables to this dish, such as potatoes, carrots, or cauliflower. Just add them to the skillet along with the chicken and cook until tender.
